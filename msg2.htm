Fish Recipes
Trout Salmon
Place fish in ziploc baggie, add 1/4 cup Dakota Buckaroo Marinating Sauce. Let set for a few minutes, turning occasionally, BBQ or grill. Marinade fillets in Dakota Buckaroo Marinating Sauce for 1 to 2 minutes, turning occasionally. Cook as desired.
BBQ Shrimp Sauteed Shrimp
Slide shrimp onto skewers and brush lightly with "Hot and Spicy" Dakota Buckaroo Marinating Sauce. BBQ or grill. Saute shrimp in frying pan with small amount of "Hot and Spicy" Dakota Buckaroo Marinating Sauce.
