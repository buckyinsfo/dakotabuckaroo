Tacos Fajitas
Add one ounce "Hot and Spicy" Dakota Buckaroo Marinating Sauce and water per pound of ground beef or ground turkey before cooking. Add about one tablespoon "Hot and Spicy" Dakota Buckaroo Marinating Sauce and water per pound of beef or chicken. Sautee till done.
Baked Beans Deviled Eggs
Add one tablespoon per quart of beans. Add approximately 1/2 tsp. to 4 eggs. Start small and add to taste!
